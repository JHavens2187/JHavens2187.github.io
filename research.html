AGN Spectral Analysis with JADES

This project focuses on analyzing the spectra of high-redshift Active Galactic Nuclei (AGN) using data from the JWST Advanced Deep Extragalactic Survey (JADES). The goal is to characterize the properties of these distant supermassive black holes.

Using Python, Astropy, and specutils, I process the raw spectra to fit emission lines and analyze their properties. This plot shows an example of a spectral fit for an AGN at a redshift of z > 5.
