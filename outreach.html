Undergraduate Teaching Assistant (UGTA)

As a UGTA for the KU Physics & Astronomy Department, I assist with introductory astronomy courses. My responsibilities include leading lab sections, helping students with observational projects, and grading assignments, all to help foster a better understanding of the cosmos.

Society of Physics Students (SPS)

I am an active member of the KU chapter of the Society of Physics Students. We work to build a community for physics majors by hosting social events, organizing panels with faculty and alumni, and conducting outreach events for local schools to promote physics education.

Public Observing Night Volunteer

I frequently volunteer at the KU Physics & Astronomy Department's public observing nights. At these events, I operate telescopes for members of the Lawrence community, answer questions about astronomy, and share the excitement of observing objects like the Moon, planets, and distant nebulae.
