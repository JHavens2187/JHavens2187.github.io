Teaching & Outreach

Inspiring the next generation of physicists and astronomers.

Undergraduate Teaching Assistant (UGTA)

As a UGTA for the KU Physics & Astronomy Department, I support courses like Stellar Astronomy. I grade assignments, provide one-on-one tutoring, and clarify complex topics for students. My success has led to multiple faculty requests for my return as a TA.

Society of Physics Students (SPS)

As an active member of the KU chapter, I assist with leadership tasks and co-lead logistics for conference attendance. I help organize social events, faculty panels, and outreach for local schools to build a strong physics community.

Public Observing Night Volunteer

I operate telescopes for the community during KU's public observing nights, helping visitors see the Moon and planets. I also lead discussions on Black Holes and Cosmology following planetarium shows.
